# Deployment & Scalability

AI4I-Orchestrate is designed for high-scale, multi-tenant deployment.

* Supports millions of requests per day
* Handles high concurrent throughput
* Enables multi-region deployments
* Integrates autoscaling for compute-intensive workloads

The platform can be deployed on sovereign infrastructure, private clouds, or hybrid environments while maintaining strict tenant isolation and policy enforcement.
